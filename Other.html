Symbols in Celtic Culture

Other

This symbol is known as the Claddagh. It is usually found on rings and is derived from an ancient legend. It is a token of friendship, loyalty, and romantic love.
The hands represent friendship. The heart represents love. The crown represents loyalty. As a wedding ring it is worn on the left hand with the heart pointed inward.
As an engagement ring it is worn on the right hand with the heart pointed inward.
And for friendship, it is worn on the right hand with the heart facing outward.
